The Pantry

The Pantry was created in an attempt to prevent unnecessary food waste after event where there is a large amount of food leftover. Events such as club meetings usually have a surplus of food leftover which often goes to the trash if no one claims it. But what if there is someone nearby, who is hungry and can take the leftovers. That’s where “The Pantry” comes into play.
